Admission & Discounts

$5 After 5pm (Fridays)

Every Friday Museum-goers receive discounted $5 general admission after 5pm. Philbrook Members always receive free general admission. Advance, timed entry tickets recommended. Tickets available for reservation online or by phone at 918-748-5300. Not a Member? Join today!

Member Colleges

Students from Member Colleges receive free admission every day with valid ID. Advance, timed entry tickets recommended. Tickets available by phone at 918-748-5300.

  • Northeastern State University (Broken Arrow)
  • Northeastern State University (Tahlequah)
  • Oral Roberts University (Tulsa)
  • Oklahoma State University (Tulsa)
  • University of Oklahoma (Tulsa)
  • Rogers State University
  • Tulsa Community College
  • University of Tulsa

Want to become a member college? Contact our Development department at (918) 748-5320 to inquire about membership benefits for your institution.
